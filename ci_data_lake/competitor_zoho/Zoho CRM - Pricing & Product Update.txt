Zoho CRM - Pricing & Product Update (December 2025)
---------------------------------------------------

Effective January 10, 2026, Zoho CRM will introduce new pricing tiers and bundled AI automation packs.

New Plans:
- Professional ($25/user/mo): Includes Blueprint, SalesSignals, and Workflow Automation.
- Enterprise ($42/user/mo): Adds Zia AI 2.0, Canvas Customization, Multi-level Territory Management.
- Ultimate ($55/user/mo): Now includes 1M API calls/month and free access to Zoho Analytics.

Key Updates:
1. Zia Credits Removed — AI usage will now be unlimited within the Enterprise plan.
2. API Limit Increase — Enterprise plan customers now get 2x more API calls.
3. Enhanced Integration with Zoho Books and Zoho Campaigns.

Strategic Focus:
Zoho aims to target mid-sized and manufacturing businesses currently frustrated with HubSpot’s AI credit pricing system.
