HubSpot Sales Hub - Feature List (Q1 2025)
------------------------------------------
Sales Hub Professional ($100/user/mo):
- Core CRM: Contact, Deal, and Pipeline Management
- Sales Automation: Sales sequences, task automation
- Reporting: Custom reporting and dashboards
- Quoting: Build and send quotes
- AI Features (Breeze AI):
  - AI Content Writer (Emails, blogs)
  - Predictive lead scoring

Sales Hub Enterprise ($150/user/mo):
- All Pro features
- Advanced Forecasting
- Playbooks
- Conversation Intelligence (Call analysis)
